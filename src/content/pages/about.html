About TechDot

Tech Dot was founded by Dominic Digiacomo in Raleigh, North Carolina, with the vision of helping small businesses thrive in the digital world. Starting from humble beginnings as a one-person operation, Tech Dot has grown into a trusted web development and consulting company. Despite our small start, we’ve always been driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to delivering high-quality, personalized digital solutions.

Today, we specialize in custom web design, SEO optimization, and digital strategy, empowering businesses to build strong online presences and achieve measurable growth. We understand the challenges small businesses face because we’ve been there, and we use that experience to provide tailored services that meet our clients' unique needs.

I’ve always had a passion for helping small businesses grow and succeed, which is why I started Tech Dot. I understand the challenges small businesses face in today’s digital landscape, and I’m committed to providing web solutions that not only meet their needs but help them thrive. Every project I take on is driven by my desire to empower businesses with the tools and strategies they need to reach their full potential and grow their online presence.

Dominic Digiacomo Founder
